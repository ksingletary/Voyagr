Rome Package

Ready to embrace 'La Dolce Vita' in the Eternal City? Our Rome package is your passport to pasta, piazzas, and a whole lot of 'wow'! Dive into history at the Colosseum, toss a coin into the Trevi Fountain for good luck, and savor every gelato swirl as you wander through charming cobblestone streets.

One of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World

Rome, the Eternal City, stands as a living testament to centuries of history, art, and unparalleled grandeur. It's a city where ancient ruins intertwine seamlessly with modern life, offering a captivating blend of past and present. Stroll through its labyrinthine streets, and you'll find yourself immersed in a tapestry of iconic landmarks—the majestic Colosseum, a behemoth from ancient gladiatorial contests and the breathtaking Pantheon, both marvels of architectural ingenuity

City Tour

Join me on an enchanting stroll through the city centre as we visit some of the major sights that you really shouldn't miss! After getting to know each other, we will move to Trajan's Column and Piazza Venezia and I will begin to explain the ancient Imperial Forum, the unification of Italy, and the rebirth of Rome during the Renaissance. The Trevi Fountain attracts millions of visitors every year and with me, you will find out why! Admire its beauty as I give you the full story behind the fountain and we get to the bottom of the coin-throwing tradition. The next stop will be in Piazza della Rotunda, the location of the world-famous Pantheon. Experience this most important of Rome’s pagan temples and discover its dynamic uses over 2000 years!

Designer Dinner Next to The Beautiful Castel Sant’Angelo

Join the latest culinary trend of immersive and social dining where local hosts invite you into their homes for a magical meal. Dine on quintessential Italian cuisine paired with delicious wines and digestifs chosen by your host. Phoebe is a brilliant visual artist, whose skills carry into her talent for creating beautiful plates of food. She will serve dishes including freshly made pasta, osso uco, chicken marsala and cooks with seasonal and local produce. our meal will be served on her terrrace, which boasts an an incredible view of the Castel Sant'Angelo!

Eat, pray, love in Rome

Discover Rome as Julia Roberts did in the movie Eat Pray Love. We will walk through the city, exploring the famous places where the movie was developed. Driven by the atmosphere of Rome, we will feel the city on our skin and delight in its romanticism. The tour will reveal the thousand-year history of the city, from its legendary birth to the glorious time of the emperors to the present day.
